Smokey Barbecue Oven Pork Ribs

Description

These will be the best ribs you have ever had! The best thing is that you don't even need a fancy smoker to make these championship tasting ribs.

Barbecue Rub

  • 2 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Ribs

  • 2.5kg pork ribs 2 racks
  • 3/4 cup sweet alcoholic apple cider
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Barbecue Sauce

  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups ketchup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 tbsp molasses
  • 1/3 cuo brown sugar
  • 2 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Combine the Rub ingredients and rub onto both sides of the ribs (most on meaty side). Set aside to marinate for 20 minutes (or overnight).
  2. Preheat oven to 160°C
  3. Place ribs on a tray in a single layer. Pour apple cider underneath the ribs, cover with foil then bake for 1 hour 30 minutes or until the meat is pretty tender
  4. Remove from oven, turn up to 180°C/350°F. Remove foil, drizzle with olive oil, then return ribs to oven for 15 minutes or until rub becomes nice and crusty.
  5. Line a new tray with foil then baking paper.
  6. Remove ribs from oven, transfer to lined tray. Pour any juices from tray over the ribs.
  7. Flip ribs so the bonier side is up. Slather with Barbecue Sauce, then bake 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from oven, then turn ribs over so the meaty side is up. Slather with Barbecue Sauce, bake 5 minutes. Repeat 2 or 3 more times until you've got a thick glaze on the ribs.
  9. Cut ribs into individual or multiple rib portions and serve with remaining Barbcue Sauce!

Barbecue Sauce Stpes

  1. Place all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 45 minutes or until thickened.
  2. Adjust to taste: sweetness with brown sugar / honey, salt and sour with vinegar. Use for glazing ribs and as a sauce for serving.
